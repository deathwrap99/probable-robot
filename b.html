What is gaming? Gaming is playing an electronic video game, which is often done on a dedicated gaming console, PC or smartphone. People who often play video games are called gamers.

Steam

Steam Workshop is a platform where you can submit, find, rate, and download new content and modifications for your favorite Steam games. You can also browse, create, and sell player-generated content for various games, such as Skyrim, XCOM 2, and Cities: Skylines.

Call of Duty

Call of Duty is a video game series and media franchise published by Activision, starting in 2003. The games were first developed by Infinity Ward, then by Treyarch and Sledgehammer Games. The series has sold over 400 million copies and is the best-selling first-person shooter game series of all time.

Rust

Rust is a multiplayer survival game where you have to fight for your life on a hostile island full of wildlife and other players. You can buy the game, watch the trailer, and get the latest news and updates from the official website of Facepunch, the developer of Rust.
